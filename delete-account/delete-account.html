Account Deletion Information - RidesKe

We're sorry to see you go! When you request to delete your RidesKe account, we want you to be fully informed about what data will be removed from our systems.

Data We Delete

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, email address, and profile photo
  • Account Settings: All preferences, saved locations, and app settings
  • Payment Information: Any saved payment methods and billing details
  • Ride History: Records of your past rides and destinations
  • Communications: Customer support interactions and feedback

Data Retention

For legal compliance and security purposes, we may retain certain information for a limited period after account deletion, including:

  • Transaction records required for tax and financial reporting
  • Information needed to prevent fraud or abuse
  • Data required by law enforcement or regulatory requirements

Note: Complete details about our data retention practices can be found in our Privacy Policy.

How to Request Account Deletion

Email us at rideske2@gmail.com. Include your phone number and mention "RidesKe Account Deletion" in the subject line.

Once we receive your request, your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
